What is HLO?

"Uhealth" Library

Uhealth is your Health wants and needs program!

Our services program offers a subsidized healthcare cost for general full laboratory services and radiology imaging procedures. We encourage our partners and individuals whose wants or needs are in health and care at a lower market price and even if your insurance has you covered with your expenses, you can still avail a discounted amount from your co-pay. We make sure you get more by paying less. Great value for you and for the hard earned money that you and your family deserve.
